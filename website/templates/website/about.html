Our Mission

Advera Labs was founded on a simple observation: ad platforms optimize for what you tell them to optimize for, not what actually matters to your business.

While Meta and Google have sophisticated Smart Bidding algorithms, they're limited by:

  • On-platform behavior data only
  • Last-click attribution models
  • Optimization goals you configure (which are often wrong)
  • No cross-platform coordination

We built Advera Labs to bridge this gap‚Äîfeeding platforms better signals, optimizing at the business level, and coordinating budgets across channels for maximum profit.
